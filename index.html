Hi, my name is Armando Garcia and I am a visual designer based in Austin, TX.

I graduated with a Communication Design degree in 2014. After completing internships, I worked as a Junior Designer at an agency that specializes in building brands with creative and public relations campaigns. I gained skills in art direction, advertisement, marketing, print and digital production, and wordpress, meanwhile working with tight deadlines and a range of clients.

My latest goal is to complete Udacity's Front-End Web Developer Nanodegree program. I am determined to add web development to my skills set.
