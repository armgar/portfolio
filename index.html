About me

About Me

I graduated with a Communication Design degree in 2014. After completing several internships, I worked at an agency that specializes in building creative campaigns, brand identities and public relations. I have since gained more experience with project management and digital production at an advertising agency. Deadlines were tight and the projects varied between clients in non-profit, art, education, entertainment and service industries.

My latest goal is to gain more experience in Interaction Design. I am learning Javascript and getting practice with PHP. I am determined to add web development to my skills set.
