A bit about me

I graduated with a Communication Design degree in 2014. After completing several internships, I worked at an agency that specializes in building creative campaigns, brand identities and public relations. Deadlines were tight and the projects varied between clients in non-profit, art, education and entertainment industries. I created branding and marketing for events, digital and print ads and publications alongside the designer and directors.

My latest goal is to complete Udacity's Front-End Web Developer Nanodegree program by the end of summer. I am determined to add web development to my skills set.
