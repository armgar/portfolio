Project

The brand Snapshot developed as a means to curate local artists, musicians and writers outside of the gallery setting. Snapshot Magazine was published and debuted for sale in February 2014. The digitally printed publication is a journey into the literary, photographic and artistic subculture in Austin, Texas.

Preliminary Observations

Initial stage of discovery and investigation in publication design lead to a collection of inspirational images. The images portray varying treatments of layout, binding and covers. The exploration led to an initial color pallette and rough logotype.

A collection of editorial design examples showcasing cover, title page and layout treatments.
